Club

Tartu Rock Climbing Club Crux is an NPO that was established on the 26th of October 2009. Our goal is to poularize and develop rock climbing as a sport and leisure activity in Tartu as well as in Estonia. Rock climbing is an activity that offers diverse opportunities for physical and mental development for people of all ages.

Various club activities that support our goals:

  • regular trainings
  • club evenings, where climbing topics are discussed
  • joint trips to rocks and boulders in Estonia and elsewhere
  • climbing competitions
  • other joint events

Club management
The articles of association of NPO Tartu Rock Climbing Club Crux state that during the period between general assemblies the club is run by the the board that has from 3 to 5 members. Members

We encourage all member applicants to contribute their time to attain the goals of our club. New members are accepted throughout the entire year if they have base knowledge of safe climbing and belaying techniques and they know how to use them in practice. Member applicants must fill an appliction form and send it to the board. Member applicants must state that they have read the articles of association and they must provide a brief explanation about motivation to become a member. Applications are accepted in electronic form or in paper form if applicants present them in training classes or at club events. Membership fees, donations etc. may be paid to our club account EE191700017002718448 (Luminor Bank).

Yearly membership fees have to be paid by all members. The fee is €10 for students and €15 for everybody else.

Download membership application


Membership benefits:

  • A chance to contribute to the development of rock climbing in Estonia (through Estonian Climbing Association)
  • A chance to purchase club clothing and other merchandise
  • A chance to take part in club events
  • A chance to participate in club climbing trips
  • A chance to rent club equipment (ropes, quickdraws, carabiners, topos etc.)
  • A chance to get a discount at Estonian hiking/climbing stores

Activities

Activities undertaken by our members:

  • Organising climbing competitions at the main climbing wall in university sports centre (in spring and autumn)
  • Structuring Estonian climbing landscape via the membership in Estonian Climbing Association
  • Taking part in national and international projects
  • Organising climbing sessions and other events
